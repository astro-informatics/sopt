Detailed Description

template<class SCALAR>
class sopt::algorithm::ProximalADMM< SCALAR >

Proximal Alternate Direction method of mutltipliers.

\(\min_{x, z} f(x) + h(z)\) subject to \(Φx + z = y\). \(y\) is a target vector.
