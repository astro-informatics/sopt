Detailed Description

template<class SCALAR>
class sopt::algorithm::PrimalDual< SCALAR >

Primal Dual Algorithm.

\(\min_{x, z} f(x) + h(z)\) subject to \(Φx + z = y\). \(y\) is a target vector.
